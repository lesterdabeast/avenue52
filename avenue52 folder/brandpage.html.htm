Ralph Lauren

Ralph Lauren is a global luxury lifestyle company founded in 1967 by designer Ralph Lauren. The brand is known for its classic American style and produces clothing, accessories, fragrances, and home furnishings. Headquartered in New York City, the company operates stores around the world and is especially known for its Polo Ralph Lauren collections.

Nella Vetrina

Nella Vetrina is a luxury furniture and lighting retailer based in New York City. The company specializes in high-end Italian design and works with European manufacturers to provide furniture and lighting for interior designers, architects, and luxury residential projects.

Neiman Marcus

Neiman Marcus is an American luxury department store chain founded in 1907 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas. The retailer sells high-end fashion, beauty products, designer accessories, and luxury home décor through both physical stores and online shopping platforms.

Of Iron & Oak

Of Iron & Oak is a boutique furniture brand known for handcrafted pieces made from solid wood and metal. The company focuses on durable materials and combines rustic, industrial, and modern design influences for residential interiors.

Audo Furniture

Audo Copenhagen is a Danish design brand producing furniture, lighting, and interior accessories inspired by Scandinavian minimalism. The brand focuses on timeless design, functional elegance, and collaborations with international designers.

B&B Italia

B&B Italia is a luxury Italian furniture manufacturer founded in 1966. The company is internationally recognized for modern furniture design and innovative manufacturing techniques. Its furniture is widely used in luxury homes, offices, and hospitality spaces.
